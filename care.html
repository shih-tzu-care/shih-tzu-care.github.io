Shih Tzu Care Checklist

Use this page as the short checklist view for temperature, routine, feeding, skin care, and training priorities.

Daily basics

  • Monitor breathing comfort after walks.
  • Keep meals measured and predictable.
  • Check skin folds and paws quickly in the evening.
  • Use short walks and calm recovery windows.

Weekly review

  • Review body condition and treat volume.
  • Clean ears if needed.
  • Check harness fit and rubbing points.
  • Adjust routines before small issues become habits.
